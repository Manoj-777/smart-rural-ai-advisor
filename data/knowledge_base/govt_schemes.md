## 1. PM-KISAN — Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi — ✅ EXAMPLE (fully filled)

### Overview
- **Launched:** February 2019 by Government of India
- **Ministry:** Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers' Welfare
- **Objective:** Direct income support to all landholding farmer families
- **Amount:** ₹6,000 per year in 3 equal installments of ₹2,000 each
- **Payment Schedule:** April-July, August-November, December-March
- **Beneficiaries:** ~11 crore farmer families

### Eligibility
- **Who qualifies:**
  - All landholding farmer families (with cultivable land in their name)
  - All small and marginal farmers (up to 2 hectares)
  - Large farmers also eligible (changed in 2019 — no land size limit for general category)
- **Who does NOT qualify:**
  - Institutional landholders (trusts, companies)
  - Current/former MPs, MLAs, Mayors, Ministers
  - Central/State government employees (except Class IV/Multi-Tasking Staff)
  - Income Tax payers (previous assessment year)
  - Professionals like doctors, engineers, lawyers, CA with income > ₹ threshold
  - Retired pensioners with monthly pension > ₹10,000

### Documents Required
1. Aadhaar card (mandatory — linked to bank account)
2. Bank account details (Aadhaar-linked)
3. Land ownership records (Khasra/Khatauni, Patta, RoR)
4. Mobile number
5. State-specific land certificates (if applicable)

### How to Apply
1. **Online:** Visit pmkisan.gov.in → "New Farmer Registration" → Enter Aadhaar + State → Fill form → Upload documents
2. **Offline:** Visit nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or local agriculture office
3. **Through Village Level Entrepreneur (VLE):** CSC operators help with registration
4. **Check status:** pmkisan.gov.in → "Beneficiary Status" → Enter Aadhaar or account number

### Tips for Farmers
- Keep Aadhaar linked to bank account (mandatory for DBT)
- If installment is delayed, check eKYC status on portal
- Land records must be updated with Revenue Department
- Report issues at PM-KISAN helpline: 155261 or 011-24300606
